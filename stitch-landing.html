The Essence of Himalayan Soil

At Giriputar, we believe that the purest treasures come from the most untouched environments. Our turmeric is grown in the high-altitude fields of the Himalayas, where the air is thin and the water is mineral-rich from melting glaciers.

Each root is hand-harvested by local Pahadi farmers who have preserved ancient, chemical-free agricultural traditions for generations. The slow growth in cold climates concentrates the medicinal properties, resulting in a turmeric that is naturally high in Curcumin.

We sun-dry our harvest on stone courtyards before grinding it using traditional methods to ensure that the aroma and oil content remain intact for your kitchen.
